# Podcast Tour List — Post-Talk Outreach

**Goal:** After your first live keynote is recorded, leverage that video into 20 podcast appearances over 6 months. Each appearance drives tapthis.co traffic + strategy call bookings + speaker authority that books the next keynote.

**Sequence matters:** Start with relationships you already have (you host Keeping It Real — many guests reciprocate). Then work outward to warm connections. Cold pitches come last, with the sizzle reel as your credibility.

**Verify before pitching:** Podcast lineups change. Hosts change. Verify the show is still active (check last episode date) before sending a pitch.

## FOLLOW-UP CADENCE

Most podcast inquiries go unanswered on the first send. This is normal. Sequence:

- **Day 0:** Initial pitch
- **Day 5:** Follow-up with one new angle: "Forgot to mention — I also have a [specific data point or anecdote relevant to their show]."
- **Day 14:** Final soft touch: "Totally understand if this isn't a fit — if timing shifts, always happy to come on."
- **Day 90:** Re-pitch with a new angle (a new tapthis.co milestone, a new talk, a new data point)

Do not chase past three pitches on the same angle.
