## PRICING GUIDANCE (for your own reference, not for the one-pager)

Typical fee ranges for speaker like D.J. (based on market, audience size, travel):

- **Local brokerage training (50-150 agents):** $1,500–$5,000 + travel
- **Regional/state association conferences:** $3,500–$10,000
- **Major franchise conferences:** $7,500–$20,000
- **NAR or industry-flagship keynote:** $15,000–$35,000
- **Virtual-only:** ~50% of in-person

Factors that move fees up: recorded usage rights, sponsor association, custom content, additional workshops or breakouts on top of the keynote, travel logistics.

For early stages of a speaker career (especially if the goal is building a reel), taking fewer-paid or unpaid slots at high-leverage events (NAR, Inman Connect, major franchises) is often the right call because the stage time + reel footage + network compound. Don't price yourself out of the rooms that build you.
